Mwenda

Pastor and Evangelist

Pst Mwenda is a devoted shepherd with a passion for guiding souls toward spiritual growth and enlightenment. With over 7 decades of pastoral experience, he has dedicated his life to serving his congregation with compassion, wisdom, and unwavering faith. Ordained at the intersection of theology and practicality, Pst. Mwenda sermons are deeply rooted in scripture yet resonate with relevance to contemporary life challenges. His ministry is characterized by a commitment to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community where individuals from all walks of life can find solace, support, and inspiration. Whether through heartfelt counseling sessions, dynamic preaching, or meaningful outreach initiatives, Pst. Mwenda consistently strives to exemplify the love and teachings of Christ, making a profound impact on the lives he touches.
